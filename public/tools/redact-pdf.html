Frequently asked questions

Can the redacted text be recovered?

No. Matched pages are flattened to images with the text burned out, so redacted content can’t be selected, searched or extracted.

Is my sensitive PDF uploaded?

No — that would defeat the point. All detection and redaction happen in your browser.
