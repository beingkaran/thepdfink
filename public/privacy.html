Privacy Policy

Your documents are yours. We built thepdf.ink so your files never have to leave your device.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

1. The short version

  • Every PDF tool — merge, split, sign, redact, fill forms, convert and the rest — runs entirely on your device in your browser or the native app.
  • Your PDF files, their contents, signatures and form data are never uploaded to us or anyone else.
  • We do not require an account and we do not build a profile of you inside the app.
  • Our marketing and information pages (this page, About, Pricing, etc.) may use cookies for analytics and advertising, as described below.

2. Files you process

When you open a PDF or image in thepdf.ink, the file is loaded into your device's memory and processed locally using the open-source pdf-lib and PDF.js libraries. The resulting file is saved back to your device when you download or export it. At no point is the file, its text, or any data derived from it transmitted to a server. Closing the tab or app clears it from memory.

The OCR and AI Summarize tools also run entirely on your device. OCR uses the open-source Tesseract engine compiled to WebAssembly; the first time you use it, the engine and the English language model are downloaded once from a public CDN (jsDelivr) and cached by your browser. That download contains only the recognition software and model — never your document. AI Summarize analyses the text on your device with no network request at all.

3. Information we do collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the website and sell the app:

  • Anonymous usage analytics on our web pages (for example, page views and country) to understand what people find useful.
  • Purchase information when you buy the $9 lifetime license. Payments are handled by our third-party checkout provider (such as Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Ko-fi or Stripe). We receive your email address and order details from them; we never see or store your full card number.
  • Support messages you send us by email.

4. Cookies & advertising

The interactive PDF tools do not run advertising. Our informational pages may display ads served by Google AdSense to help fund the project. When ads are shown:

  • Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on someone's past visits to this website. You may also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting aboutads.info.
  • A consent banner asks your permission before any advertising cookies are set, and no ad scripts load until you accept. You can decline and still use the site.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share the limited data described above only with the service providers who help us operate — our checkout provider, analytics provider and Google AdSense — and only as needed to deliver those services.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct or delete the personal data we hold about you (essentially your purchase email and support history), and to object to certain processing. Email us and we'll help.

7. Children

thepdf.ink is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes

We'll update this page if our practices change and revise the date at the top.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@thepdf.ink or use our contact page.
