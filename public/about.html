About the project

PDF tools that respect your privacy.

thepdf.ink is a complete PDF toolkit that does all its work on your own device — never in the cloud.

Why we built it

Most "free" online PDF tools ask you to upload your documents to a stranger's server. Contracts, medical records, tax forms, IDs — sensitive files handed over just to rotate a page or fill a form. We thought that was backwards. thepdf.ink flips it: the software comes to your file, not the other way around. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is stored, nothing is tracked inside the tools.

What you can do

  • Organise — merge, split, rotate, reorder and delete pages.
  • Edit — find & replace text, edit metadata, add watermarks.
  • Sign & fill — draw or type a signature, complete fillable forms.
  • Protect — permanently redact sensitive text.
  • Convert — images to PDF, PDF pages to images, compress and more.

How it works

The tools are powered by two mature open-source libraries — pdf-lib for creating and modifying PDFs and PDF.js for rendering them — running inside your browser or the native app. The desktop and mobile apps are packaged with Tauri, which wraps the same web app in a tiny, fast, native shell for macOS, Windows, iOS and Android.
